## Why Localization Matters

English makes up only a fraction of the global internet population. By localizing your app into Spanish, French, Chinese, or Arabic, you immediately expand your potential user base by billions.

## Features of Codiner's i18n System

### 1. Centralized Translation Management
Instead of hunting through code for hardcoded strings, Codiner provides a central hub where you can manage all your app's text. You can add new languages with a single click.

### 2. Live Preview
Swap between languages in your development environment to see how your UI handles different text lengths and character sets in real-time.

### 3. Smart Fallbacks
If a translation is missing in a specific language, Codiner automatically falls back to your primary language (usually English), ensuring your users never see an empty screen.

## Best Practices for Global Apps

- **Flexible Layouts**: Remember that German words are often longer than English ones, and Arabic is read from Right-to-Left. Use Codiner's responsive layout components to handle these variances.
- **Cultural Context**: Translation isn't just about words; it's about context. Use our system to manage different date formats, currencies, and localized imagery.
